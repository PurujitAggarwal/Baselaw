Top 6 Law Schools in the World 2021 by QS
  • 1. Harvard University

    One of the best Universities in the World, Harvard Law is the best there is. Only those with top scores have a shot at acceptance.

  • 2. University of Oxford

    Arguably the best University outside of America, Oxford offers one of the most competitive law courses in the world.

  • 3. University of Cambridge

    Cambridge is a hard to get into and amazing university with just as good a law course. It is Oxford's main competitor as well.

  • 4. Yale University

    A close second to Harvard, Yale has a great Law course and boasts many famous alumni.

  • 5. Stanford University

    Stanford is a world-renowned university, so seeing it on this list isn't surprising. It is another top choice for those doing U.S. Law.

  • 6. London School of Economics

    Very well-known for its economics course, LSE is a top university and has an exceptional law course.
