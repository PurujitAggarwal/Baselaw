Different Types of Lawyers
  • Litigator

    Litigators are lawyers who specialise in taking legal action against people and organisations.

  • Solicitor

    A lawyer who prepares legal documents and represents their clients legal interests.

  • Pleader

    A lawyer who only goes to court to represent their clients.

  • Criminal Lawyer

    A lawyer who specialises in criminal law. They defend the accused.
