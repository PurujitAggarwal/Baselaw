Interview around Law Schools and Career Paths
  • Choosing the Country

    Mr. Mehta says that choosing the country where you want to practice law is the first step to choosing a Law School because this will give you the most knowledge and will increase your chance at securing a job.

  • Law Schools in Different Countries

    Mr. Mehta also spoke about the fact that different countries have different methods to teach law, especially in terms of how long it takes. For example; India can be 5-6 years and the UK is 3 years.

  • Studying in a Different Country

    It is possible to learn and practice law in different countries but is not usualy done, because there is the thought that you may not know the law as well as people educated within the country.
