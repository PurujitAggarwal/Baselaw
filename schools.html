Top 5 Law Schools in USA by QS
  • 1. Harvard University

    Harvard Law is widely regarded as the best there is. Only those with top scores have a shot at acceptance.

  • 2. Yale University

    A close second to Harvard, Yale has a great Law course and boasts many famous alumni.

  • 3. Stanford University

    One of the best universities in the world. It is another top choice for those who want to do law in the U.S.

  • 4. Columbia University

    Boasting many notable alumni such as Ruth Bader Ginsburg, Columbia University has a hard to get into and amazing law program.

  • 5. New York University

    Rankings wise the lowest on this list, NYU is well known for their arts program. Their law program is also world class.

Top 5 Law Schools in UK by QS
  • 1. University of Oxford

    A top university in the world, Oxford has some great courses and their law course is no exception. It is very hard to get into.

  • 2. University of Cambridge

    Oxford's eternal rival, Cambridge has an amazing Law prgram with many famous people having attended law school there.

  • 3. London School of Economics

    Very well-known for its economics course, LSE is a top university and has an exceptional law course.

  • 4. University College London

    One of the best universities in the entire world, UCL has a top law course which any person should be happy to be a part of.

  • 5. King's College London

    Another top university, King's is slightly easier to get into than the others universities on this list but is still very good.

Other Top Law Schools by QS
  • National University of Singapore

    The most competitive college in the world, NUS has amazing programs in most subjects and law is no exception.

  • University of Melbourne

    Ranked 11, University of Melbourne has a good law program and will be ideal for someone who wants to be in Australia.

  • University of New South Wales

    Another university located in Australia, University of New South Wales is not far behind Melbourne.

  • University of Sydney

    The last Australian University to feature on this list, University of Sydney is ranked #14, ahead of UCL and King's.

  • University of Toronto

    A university on par with most of the others mentioned, University of Toronto has a good program for Law.
