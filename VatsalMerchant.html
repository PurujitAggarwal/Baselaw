Interview around the Day to Day Job of a Solicitor
  • What a Real-Estate Solicitor Does

    Mr. Merchant is involved in a lot of real estate work. He works on advising clients on property related matters, drafting documents and agreements around real estate along with handling any disputes.

  • What Else has Mr. Merchant been Involved In

    In the 1990's until 2002 Mr.Merchant was involved in filing suits against the Bombay Muncipal Corporation. He stopped because he started having a conflict of interest.

  • Why Real-Estate Solicitors are important

    All major buildings and other types of real-estate projects need a someone like Mr. Merchant to help the project complete without any issues.
