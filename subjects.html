IB
  • HL English Literature

    Highly recommended if you are interested in Law. Will help you in writing and reading which are needed in many fields of law.

  • HL History

    Can help you a lot with writing, thinking about arguments and reading a lot about one topic.

  • HL Economics

    If you want to go into law knowing a bit of finance can be useful. Economics can be a good choice.

  • SL Maths

    A requirement in IB, Maths as an SL will be good because it will let you do a subject related to Law at HL level.

  • SL Physics

    A science is also needed in IB and you can choose any but Physics is probably your best option.

  • SL Foreign Language

    IB mandates studying a foreign language, you can choose whichever foreign language you like for the final spot.

A Levels
  • English

    Helps your essay-writing and communication skills which are crucial to being a good lawyer.

  • History

    Can help you a lot with writing, thinking about arguments and reading a lot about one topic.

  • Politics

    Helps you understand the political world thanks to which legal systems have changed.

  • Maths

    Not conventional but Maths could be a good choice because it is well regarded and can help your analytic skills.
